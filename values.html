Our values

Our cultural values are aspirational. They describe the culture that the work of the Holy Spirit fosters in us as disciples of Jesus Christ. These values grow out of the gospel of grace, which is the power of God for salvation, and they find their ultimate expression in the person of Jesus Christ, the crucified, risen, ascended Lord.

Transformational Discipleship

From safe to surrendered

The gospel brings about transformation that leads to maturity in Christ, making it possible for us to love the Lord our God with heart, soul, mind and strength.

Radical Generosity

From self to others

The gospel compels us to be radical in our generosity and compassionate in how we respond to all those we encounter.

Authentic Community

From isolated to connected

The gospel enables us to genuinely grow into community and model what it means to be in community.

Missional Living

From consumer to mission

The gospel calls us to be a community of disciples on mission together, as we live out our faith in word and deed, locally and globally.

Our vision for 2025

GWAC will be a light on the hill as we live in step with Christ. We will nurture and equip one another, as we serve the broader community with a Christ-centred and Spirit-led approach.

Our missional purpose

To bring glory to God by being a caring community making, maturing and mobilising disciples of Jesus Christ, locally and globally.
