Youth

At Glen Waverley Anglican Church we have an enthusiastic team of leaders who provide oversight of IGNITE - our GWAC Youth ministry. These leaders are all passionate about encouraging young people to grow in their understanding of Jesus and how he can impact their lives for eternity and to equip them to live lives on mission in their everyday lives.

The youth ministry at GWAC is for everyone in Gr 6- Yr 12. It meets on Friday nights as a large group for worship and outreach, and is divided into smaller groups for community time. These smaller groups meet in various houses around the Glen Waverley area.

We also run various camps throughout the year, so scroll down and take a look!

Meet the leaders

Meet the leaders The youth leaders at Glen Waverley Anglican Church are second to none. Every leader is a committed Christian and seeks to live a life that pleases God.

All leaders receive extensive training and mentoring from the paid staff at Glen Waverley Anglican Church.
