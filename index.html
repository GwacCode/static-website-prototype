About us

We are a community of people, following Jesus Christ and are committed to helping others become followers of Jesus.

We seek to help each other live life together on mission as we demonstrate the reality of the rule and reign of God, on earth, as it is in Heaven. Simply put, our story is really HIS story, intersecting with our lives and bringing us individually and collectively, into relationship with himself.
